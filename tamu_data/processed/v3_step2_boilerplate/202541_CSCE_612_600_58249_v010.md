[16.0pt] Course Description
[12.0pt] This class is an overview of computer networks with a focus on Internet
[12.0pt] protocols and high-performance coding in Windows.
[16.0pt] Course Prerequisites
[12.0pt] CSCE 315 (programming studio), CSCE 313 (computer systems), CSCE 312
[12.0pt] (computer organization), CSCE 221 (data structures and algorithms), and
[12.0pt bold] most importantly  fluent knowledge of C/C++ .
[16.0pt] Special Course Designation
None
[16.0pt] Course Learning Outcomes
[12.0pt] At the end of the semester, the students will obtain experience with protocol
[12.0pt] design, Internet operation, C/C++ network APIs, and multi-threading in
[12.0pt] Windows.
