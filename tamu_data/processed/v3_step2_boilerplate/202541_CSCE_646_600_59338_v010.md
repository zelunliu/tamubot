[16.0pt] Course Description
[10.5pt] Tools and techniques for generation, handling and analysis of two dimensional
[10.5pt] digital images; image representation and storage; display, media conversion,
[10.5pt] painting and drawing; warping; color space operations, enhancement, filtering and
[10.5pt] manipulation.

[16.0pt] Course Learning Outcomes
Upon completion of this course, the learner will be able to:
identify the state of the art in the treatment of digital images in the context of
computer graphics, and digital effects;
identify essential digital images algorithms;
identify existing systems for storage, display, transformation and
manipulation of digital images;
Identify fundamental theoretical and practical aspects of 2D Computer
Graphics;
apply digital images techniques to write their own software for working with
digital images;
apply digital images techniques to undertake creative work and research
involving digital images.
