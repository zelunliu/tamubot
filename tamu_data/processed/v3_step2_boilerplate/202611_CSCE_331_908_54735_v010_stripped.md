Peer Teacher Central
Peer Teacher Central  (PTC) provides a group of students you can talk
to for help in 100, 200, and 300 level CSCE courses. Sometimes it is
more comfortable talking to a fellow student instead of the instructor
or TA. You can get help from students who have successfully
completed this course in person on the first floor of the Peterson
building in Room 127 or after hours on their discord server. You will
also have access to peer teachers in the lab. Students consistently rate
peer teachers as having a positive impact on their academic
performance.

has trained peer consultants available to work with you on any kind of
writing or speaking project, including research papers, lab reports,
application essays, or creative writing, and at any stage of your
process, whether you're deciding on a topic or reviewing your final
draft. You can also get help with public speaking, presentations, and
group projects. You can schedule in-person and Zoom appointments,
